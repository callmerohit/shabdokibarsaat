Welcome to Shabdon ki Barsaat

Hello, My name is Gautam Kataria, a passionate poet from Ferozpur, Punjab. I find joy in expressing emotions through the beautiful languages of Hindi and Punjabi. Writing poetry is not just a hobby for me but a heartfelt journey where I pour my soul into words. My verses capture the essence of love, life, and the vibrant culture of Punjab. Join me on this poetic journey and discover the magic of my words, crafted with devotion and a profound connection to my roots.
